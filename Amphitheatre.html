Amphitheatre

The amphitheatre is a large open-air venue designed for hosting events, performances, and gatherings. It features tiered seating that provides excellent views of the stage or performance area from all angles.

With its spacious layout and modern amenities, the amphitheatre is ideal for concerts, theatrical performances, and community events.

Visitors can enjoy a variety of events throughout the year, making it a vibrant hub of entertainment and culture.
