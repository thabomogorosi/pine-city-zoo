Monkey-Trail

Take a walk or ride through this challenging obstacle course. The Monkey-Trail is designed to test your agility and endurance while providing a fun and adventurous experience.

Navigate through various obstacles, including rope swings, balance beams, and climbing walls. The trail is suitable for all ages and skill levels, making it a perfect family activity.

Whether you're looking for a thrilling adventure or a leisurely stroll, the Monkey-Trail offers a unique outdoor experience.
