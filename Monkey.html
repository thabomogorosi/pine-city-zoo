Mankey

Mankey is a small, monkey-like Pokémon known for its mischievous and playful nature. It has a light brown body with a cream-colored face and a long tail. Mankey is known for its agility and speed, making it a formidable opponent in battles.

Mankey is often found in forests and jungles, where it lives in groups called troops. It is known for its social behavior and often engages in playful antics with its companions.

In battles, Mankey is known for its quick attacks and agility, making it a challenging opponent for trainers.

Mankey evolves into Primeape, a larger and more powerful Pokémon, when it reaches a certain level of experience.

Overall, Mankey is a fun and energetic Pokémon that is loved by trainers for its playful personality and impressive speed.
